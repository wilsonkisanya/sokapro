How To Bet

Normal BET

Normal Jackpot is based on seventeen (13) pre-selected football games.

The Mega Jackpot amount, consolation prizes and/or any bonuses thereof will be divided equally amongst all the Mega Jackpot winners.

To play Make your selections – On the OdiBets Mega Jackpot competition entry page (odibets.com/jackpot), make your predictions for each one of the listed matches (Home win, Draw, Away win). The stake amount of each combination is KES 95. You can make more than 1 prediction for one match. This will increase the stake amount by KES 95 for every each additional combination formed by your selections.

The Mega Jackpot bet can be placed by submitting predictions of the result of 17 pre-selected football matches (the "Matches") at OdiBets.com/jackpot or through; a. a manual option where you select each prediction i.e. SMS “JP” followed by the 17 predictions to 29680. E.g. MJP#1X21X21X21X21X212 you will receive a confirmation SMS for your bet with the possible Mega Jackpot win, once the bet is placed. ORb. an ‘Auto Bet’ method as follows SMS “MJP#?” to 29680. The OdiBets System then randomly selects the 17 predictions for you. You will receive a confirmation SMS for your bet with the possible Mega Jackpot win, once the bet is placed.c. By choosing the “Auto Bet” option, a random selection of one prediction per match will be automatically selected with a fixed stake of KES 95. Further predictions may be added to the random selection. This will increase the cost of the entry by KES 99 for every additional line. However, additional selections made on more than one match will cause the stake to increase depending on the combinations selected.
